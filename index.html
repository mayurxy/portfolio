Brown Sugar Babies

Developed web plaform for a non profit organization.

Brown Sugar Babies is a nonprofit organization helping young mothers and pregnant teens. The organization provide resources for teen moms transitioning into adult life, which includes basic necessities and life skills. Support and guidance is also provided by our caring mentors as mom journeys through the challenges of motherhood. Brown Sugar Babies serve young moms in the community between the ages of 12 and 17.

I was responsible for developing donation platform and connecting the front end to all payment gateways. Added Volunteer registration form too.
